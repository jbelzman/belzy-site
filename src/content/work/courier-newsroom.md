## The brief

Courier ran a network of eight owned media properties (Up North News, Keystone, Gander, Dogwood, Floricuas, Courier, Copper Courier, Cardinal &amp; Pine, Americano). Every property had its own voice, content cadence, and audience strategy — but no unified view of engagement, growth, or pacing. And little budget for a commercial BI stack.

## The approach

Designed, built, and flew the plane. Engineered an end-to-end newsroom analytics stack on Google Cloud Platform.

Key moves:

- **GCP-native architecture** — Looker (Data Studio) dashboards on top of BigQuery, with pipelines from WordPress, Sprout Social, Mailchimp, and voter file data.
- **Multi-property design** — all 8 publications in a single dashboard with filter-by-property and network-rollup views.
- **Four-horizon pacing** — weekly, monthly, quarterly, and YTD goal tracking with radial gauges and per-property bar breakdowns.
- **Engagement + growth unified** — social engagements, audience follower growth, video views, and email in one report.
- **B2B storytelling** — the dashboard was used directly in investor conversations and editorial strategy reviews. Built once, served two very different audiences.

## The outcome

Drove audience acquisition and engagement above forecast, supporting private investment decisions. Tied content engagement directly to audience strategy — the editorial team could see what was working and adjust the next day, not the next quarter.
